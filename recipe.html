English Scones Recipe

  • Time: 30 minutes
  • Serves: 6 serves

Ingredients

Items Quantity
Self-raising flour 3 1/2 cups (450g)
Butter 4 tablespoons
Sugar 2 tablespoons
Baking powder 2 teaspoons
Salt 1/4 teaspoon
Milk 1 1/4 cup (250mL)
Egg yolk 1

Methods

Step 1: Preheat the oven to 425˚F (220˚C).

Step 2: In a large bowl, rub together the self-raising flour and cubed butter until you form fine crumbs.

Step 3: Mix in the sugar, baking powder, and salt, and make a well in the middle.

Step 4: Pour in the milk and gently mix together until just combined.

Step 5: Tip the dough onto a floured surface and knead for about a minute. Be careful not to over-knead the dough.

Step 6: Using a round cookie cutter or the rim of a glass, cut circles into the dough that are about 1-2 inches thick.

Step 7: Place onto a baking tray lined with parchment paper and coat the tops of the scones with the egg yolk.

Step 8: Bake for 12 minutes.

Step 9: Serve with clotted cream and jam.

Step 10: Enjoy!

Source: Tasty English Scones

Chocolate Brownies Recipe

  • Time: 60 minutes
  • Serves: 8 serves

Ingredients

Items Quantiy
Unsalted butter 125 g
70% Dark chocolate (broken up) 100 g
Chopped nuts (Optional) 75 g
Cocoa powder (Sifted) 40 g
Plain flour (Sifted) 1/4 Cup
Baking powder 1/2 Teaspoon
Castor sugar 180 g
Large free-range eggs 2

Methods

Step 1: Preheat your oven to 180°C.

Step 2: Line a loaf tin with greaseproof paper.

Step 3: In a large bowl over some simmering water, melt the butter and the chocolate and mix until smooth. Add the nuts, if you're using them, and stir together.

Step 4: In a separate bowl, mix together the cocoa powder, flour, baking powder and sugar, then add this to the chocolate and nut mixture. Stir together well.

Step 5: Beat the eggs with a fork and mix in to the brownie mixture until you have a silky consistency.

Step 6: Pour your brownie mix into the baking tray, and place in the oven for around 20 - 25 minutes. You don't want to overcook them so, unlike cakes, you don't want a skewer to come out all clean. The brownies should be slightly springy on the outside but still gooey in the middle.

Step 7: Allow to cool in the tray

Step 8: Enjoy!

Source: Miss Nijenhuis' Food Tech class

Chocolate Muffins Recipe

  • Time: 40 minutes
  • Serves: 12

Ingredients

Items Quantity
Plain flour 1 3/4 cups
Sugar 1 cup
Cocoa powder 1/2 cup
Baking powder 2 teaspoons
Salt 1/4 teaspoon
Large eggs (lightly beaten) 2
Milk 1 cup
Melted butter 1/2 cup
Chocolate chips (optional) 1/2 cup
Baking soda 1/4 teaspoon

Methods

Step 1: Preheat oven to 180°C and grease a muffin pan.

Step 2: Combine flour, sugar, cocoa, baking powder, baking soda and salt into a bowl.

Step 3: Add in eggs, milk and melted butter. Stir until combined.

Step 4: Add chocolate chips and mix.

Step 5: Spoon into prepared pan. Bake for 18-20 minutes.

Step 6: Transfer onto a wire rack to cool.

Step 7: Enjoy!
